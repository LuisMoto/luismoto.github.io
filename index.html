About Me

I've always been interested in how information is structured — whether through the rules of a language or the logic of an equation. My background reflects that: I hold a B.A. in Linguistics (UAM) and I am currently pursuing a B.Sc. in Applied Mathematics at UNAM.

I work in Business Intelligence and Data Analysis, mostly with Python, SQL, and Power BI. In recent projects I've also used Machine Learning for pattern detection and anomaly analysis.

A good analysis doesn't end with the code or the dashboard. It ends when someone else can understand it, question it, and use it.

Beyond the Data

Music & Books: I play bass and piano, mostly with friends. Outside of that I read a lot — I'm drawn to Latin American authors like Borges and Cortázar, works that balance structure and freedom.
