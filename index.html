About Me

I’ve always been fascinated by how we structure information, whether through the rules of a language or the logic of an equation. My trajectory reflects this idea: I hold a B.A. in Linguistics (UAM) and I am currently pursuing a B.Sc. in Applied Mathematics at UNAM.

I focus on Business Intelligence (BI) and Data Analysis, using tools like Python, SQL, and Power BI to transform disordered information into clear models and actionable decisions. In my recent projects, I have applied Machine Learning for pattern and anomaly detection, looking beyond the numbers to understand the system.

I believe a good analysis doesn’t end with the code and the dashboard. It ends when someone else can understand it, question it, and use it.
